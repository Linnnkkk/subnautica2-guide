Creature Survival Tips

  • Passive creatures won't attack but will flee. Approach slowly for scanning
  • Defensive creatures attack only when provoked. Keep your distance
  • Aggressive creatures hunt on sight. Use Seaglide to escape quickly
  • Leviathan-class — always avoid. No weapon in the game can kill them
  • Repulsion Cannon works on most medium-sized creatures for emergency escapes
  • Stasis Rifle can freeze most creatures temporarily — including Leviathans
