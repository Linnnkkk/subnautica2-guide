Material Locations

Can't find a specific resource? Every material in Subnautica 2 organized by biome, depth, and source type. Find what you need fast.

Scanner Room Tip: Build a Scanner Room and equip the HUD chip to locate specific resources nearby. It's the fastest way to find rare materials.

Common Materials (0–100m)

These are found in the Safe Shallows and Kelp Forest. You'll need lots of them throughout the game.

MaterialSourceBiomeNotes
TitaniumLimestone Outcrop, Metal SalvageSafe Shallows, everywhereMost used resource. Break limestone and pick up metal salvage.
CopperLimestone OutcropSafe Shallows, Kelp ForestNeeded for wiring and electronics. Always keep a stock.
QuartzSeafloor depositSafe Shallows, CavesUsed for glass, which is needed for most modules.
Silicone RubberCreepvine Seed ClusterKelp ForestYellow seed pods on tall kelp. Craft from Creepvine seeds.
Fiber Mesh2x Creepvine SampleKelp ForestCut Creepvine stalks with knife for samples.
LeadSandstone OutcropKelp Forest, Grassy PlateausNeeded for radiation suits and reactor shielding.

Mid-Range Materials (100–300m)

Found in deeper biomes. You'll need vehicles to collect these efficiently.

MaterialSourceBiomeNotes
SilverSandstone OutcropGrassy Plateaus, Kelp CavesUsed for Wiring Kits. Often competes with Gold drops.
GoldSandstone Outcrop, Large Resource DepositGrassy Plateaus, Sea Treader PathNeeded for Computer Chips. Save some for late game.
LithiumSeafloor, Shale OutcropMushroom Forest, Sea Treader PathPlentiful on the Sea Treader Path. Used for plasteel.
DiamondShale OutcropUnderwater Islands, Sea Treader CavesRare drop from shale. Save for laser cutter and modifications.
MagnetiteSeafloor depositUnderwater Islands, Jellyshroom CaveFound on the cave floors. Needed for scanner upgrades.
Cave SulfurSulfur Plant (after Crashfish)Kelp Caves, Safe Shallows cavesLure the Crashfish out, dodge it, then collect from the plant.

Deep Sea Materials (300m+)

These require Seamoth (depth upgrade) or Prawn Suit to collect. Plan your dives carefully.

MaterialSourceBiomeNotes
KyaniteLarge Resource DepositInactive Lava Zone, Lava LakesOnly found in lava biomes. Need Prawn Drill Arm to harvest deposits.
NickelLoose nodes, Large DepositLost River, Lava ZoneBlue-green loose nodes on the ground. Needed for depth modules.
Ion CubesAlien Bases, Precursor CachesAlien Containment facilitiesPurple glowing cubes. Used for advanced crafting and portals.
UraniniteSeafloor deposit, Large DepositLost River, deep cavesGreen radioactive ore. Needed for Nuclear Reactor rods.
Crystalline SulfurPlant nodesLost River, Lava ZoneGrows on walls in deep caves. Different from regular Cave Sulfur.
Deep Dive Warning: Always check your vehicle's depth limit before going after deep materials. A Seamoth without upgrades implodes at 200m. Get the Depth Module Mk1 (300m) first.

Farming Guide: Renewable Resources

Some resources can be farmed infinitely using Exterior Grow Beds and Alien Containments:

ResourceMethodGrowth Time
CreepvineExterior Grow Bed from seed~5 minutes
Acid MushroomExterior / Interior Grow Bed~3 minutes
Table CoralExterior Grow Bed~8 minutes
Bladderfish (water)Alien Containment + releaseBreeds over time
Peeper (food)Alien Containment + releaseBreeds over time
Efficiency Tip: Build 2-3 Exterior Grow Beds near your base with Creepvine and Acid Mushrooms. You'll never run out of basic crafting materials.
