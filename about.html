About This Site

What Is Subnautica 2 Guide?

Subnautica 2 Guide is a fan-made, community-driven resource dedicated to helping players explore and survive the alien oceans of Subnautica 2. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by Unknown Worlds Entertainment.

Our Mission

We aim to provide the most accurate, up-to-date guides for Subnautica 2 Early Access. Our content includes:

  • Comprehensive walkthroughs — step-by-step guides for every stage of the game
  • Creature database — every fauna documented with behavior, threat level, and survival tips
  • Base building guides — from your first room to advanced deep-sea outposts
  • Material locations — every resource mapped out by biome and depth
  • Crafting recipes — complete crafting trees for all tools and vehicles

Update Schedule

Subnautica 2 is currently in Early Access, which means the game changes frequently. We update our guides within 24-48 hours of each patch to ensure accuracy. Our content is based on actual in-game research, not speculation.

Last Updated: May 11, 2026 — Content reflects the Early Access launch version. All data will be updated as we explore the game after release.

Accuracy Commitment

We take accuracy seriously. All guides are based on actual gameplay and verified data. If you find an error or outdated information:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • We review and fix reported issues within 48 hours

Content Sources

Our information comes from:

  • Direct in-game research and testing
  • Official patch notes from Unknown Worlds Entertainment
  • Community contributions and corrections
